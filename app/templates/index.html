Tactification...

is an attempt to discuss and learn football tactics. When I watch football, I always try to predict next pass based on players teammates in maximum space. But never able to forecast what the pass the received player gonna pick.

Here in tactification.com, I will post some interesting movements from games and listen to viewers for their predictions for the next moves and justification for their predictions. Everyweek, will create a new post and the video of the play will be posted in tactification's youtube channel after one week.
